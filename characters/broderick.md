## Notes on Character Essence

Broderick embodies the warrior who combines brute force with surprising tactical acumen. His visual appearance should convey both raw strength and an unexpected thoughtfulness. Key elements that define his character:

- The contrast between his imposing physical presence and his slight stoop
- The glitter contamination in his beard (aftermath of Lady Prismatica encounter)
- His elaborate pre-battle stretching routine
- His tendency to name weapons and address them in battle
- His role as the party's frontline combatant and self-appointed morale officer
