# Morganthe Bloom (M0RG)

## Character Description

A human woman with dark brown skin, wild black hair containing actual wildflowers, and eyes that reflect the phases of the moon. She has a round face and perpetually smiling expression despite the horrors she's witnessed. Her movements are deliberate and graceful, suggesting both medical precision and a deep connection to natural rhythms. Her gaze combines compassion with clinical assessment, constantly evaluating the health of those around her.

## Clothing/Outfit Description

Wears robes of undyed linen embroidered with medicinal herbs that seem to shift subtly with the seasons. Features specialized pockets for experimental compounds, one specifically dedicated to "distraction enhancement chemicals" after her successful deployment during the Quadrumvirate escape. Carries various pouches containing healing herbs, alchemical ingredients, and at least three experimental compounds she's currently testing, all organized with meticulous care.

## Notes on Character Essence

Morganthe embodies the healer who combines scientific curiosity with genuine compassion. Her visual appearance should convey both her connection to nature and her enthusiasm for experimental alchemy. Key elements that define her character:

- The wildflowers growing in her black hair, suggesting her connection to nature
- Her eyes that reflect the phases of the moon
- Her perpetual smile that suggests optimism without naivety
- Her talking to plants and expecting responses
- Her role as the party's primary healer and voice of gentle reason with a growing interest in offensive alchemy
